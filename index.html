About Team SHDW

Team SHDW is a gaming organization. We have many important places such as social media content, departments to keep the team running well, and merch for people who want to support team SHDW. The team was made by Scout on October 1, 2022 and grew very quickly. We have a YouTube channel, our own Discord server, a Twitter page, and Twitch for our streamers on team SHDW.

Team SHDW Divisions

As said in the about, team SHDW has many different departments to keep our team running. We have mods for our discord, our roster, and our social media runners. These are important to our team for many different reasons.Our moderation team, ensures that we have a safe and supportive team for all of our fans around the world. This includes helping out fans and keeping the server for everyone.Our roster, well, keeps content for our fans. Without these guys there would be no team. They create content for the YouTube channel and keep us on the leaderboard for cash that goes towards our team.Last, but not least, we have the social media runners. This includes thumbnail designers for the channel, keeping the website up to date, and keeping the Twitter page up to date.Without our departments we wouldn't be a team. Please thank our staff and everyone else for this amazing team.
